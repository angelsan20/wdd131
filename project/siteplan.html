2x1 HOME - Project Site Plan

SITE DESCRIPTION

SITE NAME

2x1HOME
The name represents the goal of the site which is to share messages to support marriage and help younger couples improve their relationships through some tips.

SITE PURPOSE

The goal of this site is to provide a list of inspirational messages about marriage, marriage strengthening and couple growth to enhance the relationship, using resources such as sharing daily quotes and various messages to help all visitors to strengthen their relationship, the website includes:

  • A home page with a daily quote and 2 weekly messages.
  • A collection of messages that users can read, share online and even download to print or study offline.
  • A page to post comments or other suggested messages to add to our catalog.

SCENARIOS

  • What should I do to keep my relationship stable?
  • How can I be a better husband/wife and be more helpful in the responsibilities we share?
  • How can I show more love to my husband/wife?
  • What can I do in my marriage to avoid routine and keep us in love?
  • How do I adapt to my new home?
  • How do I adjust to the arrival of children?
